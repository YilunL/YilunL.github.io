34th Street Magazine Article About Me!

Summary

Hi! My name is Ivan Li, and I am currently a senior pursuing an uncoordinated dual degree + double major between the College of Arts and Sciences (Mathematics, Economics) and Wharton (Statistics) at the University of Pennsylvania. Starting Fall 2019 I will be pursuing a Ph.D. in Quantitative Marketing at Stanford's Graduate School of Business.

My research interests are related to Empirical/Structural Industrial Organization with a particular emphasis on pricing. My undergraduate thesis is advised by Aviv Nevo.

You may know me as Penn's highest rated and most decorated teaching assistant! I have received five teaching awards total, two for Math 114 (Spring 2017 and Fall 2017) and three for Math 240 (Spring 2018, Fall 2018, and Spring 2019).

Bio

I graduated from Brookings High School in Brookings, SD, but now call Dallas, TX my home. My hobbies include basketball, listening to music, and playing the clarinet.

I currently do research for Penn Wharton Budget Model and will TA Math 240 - Calculus III at UPenn for Spring 2019. I was a Business Analyst Intern for Capital One during the summer of 2017.

As for my hobbies, I listen to a ton of hiphop and R&B.

I follow the NBA closely; my favorite team is the Los Angeles Lakers (No I'm not a LeBron bandwagoner). You can catch me playing pickup at Pottruck occasionally!
